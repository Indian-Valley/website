Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Open the app, browse our menu, add items to your basket, and proceed to checkout. You can choose collection or delivery, select a time slot, and pay securely with a saved card.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order before the restaurant begins preparing it. Once preparation has started, changes are no longer possible. If you need help, contact us as soon as possible.

How do I track my order?

After placing your order, you'll receive real-time updates via push notifications and on the order status screen within the app.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider.

How does the loyalty programme work?

You earn points on every eligible order placed through the app. Points accumulate automatically and can be redeemed for discounts and free items. Check your points balance in the Rewards section of the app.

I have a food allergy — can I still order?

We display allergen information for each menu item in the app. However, our food is prepared in kitchens where allergens are present, and cross-contamination may occur. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us directly before ordering.

How do I update my account details?

Go to the Profile section in the app to update your name, email, phone number, delivery address, or saved payment methods.

How do I reset my password?

Tap "Forgot password" on the login screen and enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive a link to create a new password.

How do I delete my account?

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from our Account Deletion page. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

Reporting a Problem

If you experience a technical issue with the app, please include the following when contacting us so we can help you faster:

  • Your device type and operating system version (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 18).
  • A brief description of the problem.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if possible.
  • The email address associated with your account.
