What happens when you delete your account

When you request account deletion, we will permanently erase your account and all personal data associated with it. This action is irreversible. The following data will be deleted:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and delivery addresses.
  • Saved payment methods (card details held by our payment processor will also be removed).
  • Order history and preferences.
  • Loyalty points and rewards balance.
  • Push notification settings and device tokens.
  • Any other personal data linked to your account.

Before you proceed

Please be aware of the following:

  • Any outstanding orders will still be fulfilled. Account deletion will be processed after all active orders are complete.
  • Any unused loyalty points will be permanently forfeited and cannot be recovered.
  • If you later wish to use the Indian Valley app, you will need to create a new account from scratch.
  • We may retain certain anonymised or aggregated data that can no longer be linked to you, as permitted by law.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been fully deleted.
